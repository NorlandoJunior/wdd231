Lumière Bistro - Site Plan

Site Name

Lumière Bistro - The web site provides small information about a fictitious restaurant.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to:

  • Call new clients to restaurant
  • Offer a clients a new place to hangout with a good food.
  • A pleasant environment to hang out with your friends after work with great cuisine

Scenarios

  • "I want to discover a new environment that is pleasant and has good food"
  • "What would be the best place to go out and spend quality time with family?"
  • "a place with a totally different cuisine, I've never seen anything around here with the same flavor and service"

Color Schema

Primary Color: Field drab (#7A5200) - Used for headers and buttons.

Secondary Color: Anti-flash White (#EBEBEB) - Used for backgrounds.

See the coloros

Typography

Primary Font: Raleway - Used for body text.

Heading Font: Montserrat - Used for headings.
