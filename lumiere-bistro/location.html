A Glimpse into Our History and Home

Step inside Lumière Bistro and you'll find more than a restaurant — you'll discover a space rich with charm, character, and stories from the past. Once a 19th-century candle workshop, our home blends timeless ambiance with heartfelt hospitality.

Our Story

Tucked away on a charming cobblestone street, Lumière Bistro opened its doors in 1998, in what was once a 19th-century candle workshop. Legend has it that the scent of fresh herbs and melting wax would drift through the air, attracting artists and poets of old Paris. Today, that same cozy energy lives on — only now, it's the aroma of garlic confit and baked brie that welcomes you in.

Over the years, Lumière Bistro has become more than just a place to dine — it's a haven where tradition and creativity meet. The original wooden beams still frame the ceiling, echoing the soft whispers of conversations past, while vintage chandeliers cast a golden glow over hand-painted tiles brought in from Marseille. Each dish we serve is inspired by old family recipes and reimagined with a modern touch, honoring both our roots and our love for culinary artistry.

Our guests don’t just come for the food — they come for the warmth of the space, the soft jazz echoing in the background, and the feeling of stepping into a timeless story. Whether you're here for a romantic evening or a quiet moment with a glass of wine, Lumière welcomes you like an old friend returning home.
